Coco Martin
Coco Martin is one of the most accomplished and known actor in the Philippines. He is known for a lot of movies and shows but mainly known for the tv show "FPJ's Ang Probinsyano" and "Juan Dela Cruz". He has achieved awards like an ASEAN International Film Festival and Award, Two Famas Aawards, PMPC Star awards and more.
Coco Martin has also given to the residents of GRACES home for the elderly in Quezon City and gave gifts, donations along with food, drinks, and goods. He also gave caring and sweet speeches.
Martin began acting early in his life. Around his 20's he started acting and played minor roles in movies and tv shows. He made his big break on leading roles in Masahinta, Daybreak, and more.
